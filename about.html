About Us

There is an overwhelming number of pet products on the market nowadays ‚Äì you can find anything your cat or dog could possibly need online or in big-box stores. It's great to have options, but sometimes it's hard to know where to start when you're trying to keep your pet as healthy and happy as possible.

That's where we come in. Pawfect Pets was founded by animal lovers who want the absolute best for their pets. We were looking for high-quality products that served our pets and looked good doing it. We wanted a place with a curated selection of products that we could trust. When we couldn't find it, we created our own!

Each Pawfect Pets product is carefully researched, developed, and tested in-house before we offer it on our site. We wouldn't sell anything that isn't enthusiastically approved by our families, and more importantly, by our pets. We hope you're just as happy with our products as we are. And if you're not? We'll do everything we can to make it right.
